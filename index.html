// COMMON QUERIES

Frequently Asked Questions

Is penetration testing legal? +
Yes — penetration testing is 100% legal when performed with written authorization from the asset owner. We always begin every engagement with a scoped Rules of Engagement (RoE) document and signed authorization. We operate strictly within the agreed scope.
How long does a typical engagement take? +
Timelines vary by scope. A focused web app test typically takes 5–10 business days. A full red team operation can span 3–6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline estimate before the engagement begins so you can plan accordingly.
Will the testing disrupt our operations? +
We design all engagements to minimize operational impact. For production environments, we coordinate testing windows during off-peak hours. We also offer isolated staging environment testing and always have a kill-switch protocol in place.
What does the final report include? +
Every report includes an executive summary, CVSS-scored vulnerabilities, proof-of-concept evidence, business impact analysis, and step-by-step remediation recommendations. We also offer a 30-day free re-test to verify fixes.
Do you sign NDAs? +
Absolutely. We sign mutual NDAs before any scoping call or proposal. Client confidentiality is the cornerstone of our business. We never disclose client names or engagement details publicly without explicit written consent.
Can you help with compliance requirements? +
Yes. Our GRC team specializes in ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type I & II, PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, and NIST frameworks. We offer full gap assessments, policy drafting, evidence collection support, and pre-audit readiness reviews.
